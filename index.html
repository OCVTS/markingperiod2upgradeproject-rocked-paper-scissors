Rocked Paper Scissors

Decsription

  • Rocked Paper Scissors is like normal Rock Paper Scissors, but cranked up to 11. It has way more options and a few modifiers that either completely change how the game works, or add even more options.
  • NOTE: At the moment, this is more of a tech demo than an actually balanced game.

The Process

  • I had the flu and was out the week where we focused on this project, so I had to do it mostly at home! I also completely underestimated the scope of the project, causing me to spend a lot less time on it than I should've. A bunch of things had to be scrapped (at least for now) to make the due date. In addition, not all options and modifiers are fully implemented.
  • It was originally a basic Rock Paper Scissors script that ran entirely within the console. After a lot of brainstorming, staying up really late, needless procrastination, and hours of tweaking the tooltip structure to look juuust right, it reached the state of v1.0. Which is very unfinished, but meets the requirements of submission.
